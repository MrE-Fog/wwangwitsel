Biography

I am a third-year master student of the School of Computer Science and Engineering in Southeast University, China, under the supervision of Prof. Min-Ling Zhang. I got my B.Eng. degree from the Advanced Class of Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST), China in 2019. I previously worked as an intern at Alibaba Group and DiDi.

My research interests lie in the theory and applications of machine learning, including weakly supervised learning and representation learning.
