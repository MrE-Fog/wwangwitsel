Biography

I am a third-year master student of the School of Computer Science and Engineering in Southeast University, China, under the supervision of Prof. Min-Ling Zhang. I got my B.Eng. degree from the Advanced Class of Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST), China in 2019.

My research interests lie in the theory and applications of machine learning and data mining. Most recently, I am focusing on weakly supervised learning and representation learning.
