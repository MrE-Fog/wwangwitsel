Biography

I am a third year master student of School of Computer Science and Engineering in Southeast University and a member of PALM Lab, under the supervision of Prof. Min-Ling Zhang. I got my B.Eng. degree from Advanced Class of Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) in June 2019. In the same year, I was admitted to study for a master degree in Southeast University without entrance examination. I was a research intern from June, 2021 to September, 2021 at Alibaba Group, advised by Qing Da.
